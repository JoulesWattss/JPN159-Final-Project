Artist's Statement

"Video games are unusual products that differ from other industrial goods in which electrical power, machines and creative work is integrated. They are an aggregation of a variety of disciplines, including engineering, literature, the arts, psychology, and can be considered a cultural tool for the 'fun' required by society."

-Toru Iwatani, "The Pac-Man Principle: A User's Guide to Capitalism"

This interactive experience explores the intersection of classic arcade aesthetics and modern web technology. Through the lens of Pac-Man's iconic design language, we examine how simple geometric forms and vibrant colors can create compelling digital narratives.

The project serves as both homage and critique, celebrating the foundational elements of game design while questioning the boundaries between art, entertainment, and cultural expression in the digital age.
