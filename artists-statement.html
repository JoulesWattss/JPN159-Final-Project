Artist's Statement

"My interactive webpage project explores the revolutionary impact of three iconic Japanese video games that fundamentally shaped the country's game development and on a larger scale global gaming culture as a whole. I wanted to celebrate the creative vision and marketing genius that transformed these games from mere entertainment into cultural phenomena transcending time, borders and especially demographics by creating my own interactive and playful digital learning experience.
Video games represent a unique convergence of "electrical power, machines and creative work," as Toru Iwatani statesâ€”they are cultural tools that integrate engineering, literature, arts, and psychology to create the 'fun'. Each of my chosen games represents a pivotal moment in Japanese gaming history, demonstrating how creative problem-solving and cultural insight can reshape entire industries - just as we saw with 'Hello Kitty' in class. My interactive format allows users to experience these innovations firsthand, understanding not just what made these games successful, but how their design philosophies continue to influence contemporary digital culture. By making the learning process engaging and participatory, my project (hopefully, fingers crossed) literally embodies the same principles of accessibility and broad appeal that made these games revolutionary. "
